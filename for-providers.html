Tiered Access Model

Lead Distribution Flow

Leads move through three tiers. The earlier your plan, the earlier you see the lead.

First Access
Exclusive 24-hour window
See the lead before anyone else. Exclusive access window before it opens to Premium providers.

After Featured window closes

Open Access

Full marketplace access. Compete on response speed and profile quality to win the lead.
